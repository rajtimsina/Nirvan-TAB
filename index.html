Pay It Forward

Each one of us has a unique talent. I frimly believe that every single one of us has the light to change the world no matter how big or small the change might be. I had this starling realization when one of my friends persuaded me to not throw the trash on the streets. Now every time I have some wrappers on me, I am reminded me to keep it to myself. This simple act changed my world, although it may not seem to be that big of a deal. Just like my friend, I hope you inspire the good in others and to share their individual skills to the world, making it a little bearable for as long as we are here dancing this divine dance. Lets help change the world by giving back to eachother in our own individual ways.
